EDA of Blizzard's Hearthstone

This project is my first and also the first of a series of analysies of Blizzard's Hearthstone.

My goal was to understand what are the possible ratios of most of the aspect of Hearthstone, one of the most recent though popular of all online collectible card games.

The Project involved : Web Scrapping, API calling, Automation, Transformation, Cleaning, Selection, Visualization, Analysis, Rmarkdown & Shiny.
